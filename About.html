About Watson Recording Studio

Watson Recording Studio is a multi‑genre production powerhouse specializing in Country Rock, Jazz, Funk, R&B, Reggae, Yacht Rock, Rock, Metal, Classical, and Big Band Orchestra. We use Cubase 15.0.21 to create full productions, arrangements, melodies, harmonies, and mixes tailored to your vision.
