About Watson Recording Studio

The story, the passion, and the journey behind the sound.

The Story Behind the Sound

Watson Recording Studio is more than a workspace — it’s the result of a lifetime shaped by rhythm, creativity, family, and the drive to bring music to life.

My musical journey started at home. When I was around four to seven years old, my dad put a guitar in my hands. He taught me how to hold chords, how to feel rhythm, and how to listen — really listen — to the heart of a song.

I joined the SGI Brass Band, one of the biggest marching groups on Oʻahu. We marched in nearly every major parade — and we didn’t just show up, we won, often beating out well‑known high schools with huge programs.

I started on snare, moved to tenor, and eventually found my home on bass drum — the heartbeat of the band. Those memories shaped who I am as a musician and as a person.

My Creative Approach

Today, I bring that same energy into Watson Recording Studio — the precision of percussion, the creativity of guitar, the discipline of marching band, and the inspiration that’s been with me since childhood.
