Every track from Watson Recording Studio is available for licensing. Whether you're creating content, producing a film, or building a brand, we offer flexible licensing options to match your project.

How Licensing Works

When you purchase a license, you receive a downloadable file containing the track and a PDF license agreement. Your license grants you the rights listed above, and you may use the track in your project immediately.

For Exclusive and Sync licenses, we will contact you directly to finalize the agreement and deliver stems if needed.
